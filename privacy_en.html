Privacy Policy – Freestyle Makerr

Address: Jerusalem, Israel

1. Introduction

“Freestyle Makerr” (the “App”) lets music lovers record freestyle vocals, play drum-pads and capture their performance. We respect your privacy and collect no personal data.

2. Data We Collect

  • No personal or analytical data is collected, stored or shared.
  • During recording, a temporary audio file is created in the App’s sandbox and deleted when the user leaves the Playback/Export screen unless shared immediately (e.g. via WhatsApp).
  • No backend server, no advertising SDKs, no analytics SDKs.

3. Device Permissions

PermissionPurposePrompt timing
RECORD_AUDIO / MicrophoneCapture freestyle vocals & drum padsFirst tap on “Record”

No other permissions are requested (storage, location, camera, etc.).

4. Third-Party Sharing

The App does not sell, rent or share any user data. No external SDKs are integrated.

5. Security

  • Audio files remain in the App’s private sandbox; no extra encryption is applied.
  • With no network traffic, interception risk is minimal.
  • The App is code-signed via Google Play App Signing and Apple App Store.

6. Children

The App targets a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. Should we learn that information was provided by a child, it will be deleted immediately.

7. Copyright

All audio, graphics and text in the App are copyright-protected. Commercial use is prohibited without prior written consent from Gaon_ai.

8. User Rights

Because we collect no personal data, there is nothing to access, correct or delete. Feel free to contact us via e-mail.

9. Changes

We may update this Policy from time to time; the new date will appear at the top. Material changes will be highlighted in-App.

10. GDPR & CCPA Compliance

The App is available worldwide. As of today no personal data is processed; should that change we will request explicit consent and update this Policy accordingly.
