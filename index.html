1. Introduction

I respect your privacy. “Freestyle Makerr” (the “App”) collects, stores and shares no personal data.

2. Data I Collect

  • I do not collect, store or share any personal or analytical data.
  • During recording, the App creates a temporary audio file inside its private cache (getTemporaryDirectory()) and deletes it when the user leaves the Playback / Export screen.
  • When the user taps “Share”, the file is copied to the App’s external cache so WhatsApp (or another target app) can read it, then deleted automatically as soon as the share sheet closes.
  • The App includes no backend server, advertising SDKs or analytics SDKs.

4. Third-Party Sharing

The App never sells, rents or shares user data. Once a recording is handed off to a third-party app (e.g. WhatsApp), its further storage and processing are governed solely by that app’s privacy policy; I have no access to, or control over, the file after hand-off.

5. Security

  • Audio files remain in the App’s sandbox and cache; no additional encryption is required because they never leave the device unless the user decides to share them.
  • I disable Android system backup (android:allowBackup="false" and android:fullBackupContent="false") so cached audio is not included in device backups.
  • Because the App exchanges no network traffic, interception risk is minimal.
  • The App is code-signed through Google Play App Signing (Android) and the Apple App Store (iOS).

6. Children

The App targets a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. If I learn that information was provided by a child, I will delete it immediately.
